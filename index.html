Events Schedule!

Here are some of our common Events

Open Water Swims

Open water swims take place in lakes, rivers, or the ocean, offering a unique and adventurous swimming experience. These events help swimmers build endurance and enjoy the beauty of nature while swimming in a group setting.

Group Swim Sessions

Group swim sessions are designed for swimmers of all levels to practice together in a supportive environment. These sessions focus on improving technique, building stamina, with Coaches to provide guidance and structured workouts, making it a great way to stay motivated and have fun while swimming.

Swim Meets

Swim meets are competitive events where swimmers can showcase their skills and compete against others. These meets are casual events for fun with ribbons and medals, providing opportunities for swimmers to achieve personal bests and earn recognition for their hard work.
